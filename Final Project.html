About Self Driving Cars


Self Driving Cars

Self Driving cars are the future to driving. All big name companies are starting to come out with new self driving cars. Such as Tesla, Google, Apple, and many more. The science behind all of this code and technology will blow your mind. Let's start with what a self Driving car is. Basically a self driving car is a car that automated by code and doesn't need a driver. Now there are 6 levels of self driving assistance. Level 0 only lets the car react to the driver's input. Level 1 is a little driving assistance such as if you are drifting into another lane it will help you straighten back into the lane you are in. Level 2 is what you would call cruise control. Level 3 the car can drive by its self but the driver must be alert. Level 4 your car can drive on its own on fimiliar roads. Level 5 your car can drive on any road under any conditions.
